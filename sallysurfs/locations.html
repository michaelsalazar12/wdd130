Surf Locations

Need help looking for your next wave? Here are some of the best surfing locations in the world! If any of these locations interest you, click the Book Now link below to get you started.

Pipeline, Oahu, Hawaii

Pipeline, located on the North Shore of Oahu, is widely regarded as one of the most iconic and challenging surf breaks in the world. Known for its powerful and barreling waves, Pipeline attracts experienced surfers from around the globe. The massive swells that roll in during the winter months create perfect conditions for tubular waves, offering an exhilarating and adrenaline-pumping experience. Watching the professionals navigate the challenging barrels is also a sight to behold. Pipeline's stunning natural beauty, warm water, and the overall surfing culture of the North Shore make it a must-visit destination for any serious surfer.

Jeffreys Bay, Eastern Cape, South Africa

Jeffreys Bay, commonly referred to as "J-Bay," is a legendary surf spot located in the Eastern Cape of South Africa. It is renowned for its flawless, long, and fast right-hand point break. The consistency of the waves, coupled with their incredible length, makes J-Bay a paradise for both professional and recreational surfers alike. The waves at Supertubes, the main break at Jeffreys Bay, can peel for up to 300 meters, offering surfers ample time to carve and perform impressive maneuvers. J-Bay's natural beauty, vibrant surfing community, and the opportunity to experience one of the world's best right-handers make it an unforgettable destination.

Uluwatu, Bali, Indonesia

Uluwatu, situated on the southwestern tip of Bali, is a tropical paradise and a surf destination of dreams. This world-class reef break boasts consistent and powerful waves, making it a favorite among experienced surfers. The main break at Uluwatu produces long and barreling left-hand waves that provide a thrilling and challenging ride. The surf breaks over a shallow reef, creating hollow tubes that are perfect for barrel riding. Beyond the incredible waves, Uluwatu is known for its stunning cliffs overlooking the Indian Ocean, beautiful beaches, and vibrant Balinese culture. The combination of epic surf, breathtaking scenery, and the unique cultural experience make Uluwatu a top choice for surfers seeking an unforgettable adventure.
